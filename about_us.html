about us

founders

Dore instruments was founded in 1912 by Ben Brown and Robert Aaron with one aim — music education.

Ben Brown

Ben Brown was born on 1890 in London. He grew up in an upper class neighbourhood. He was raised by his mother, his father having left when he was young. began his career in the music business as a retail manager

Robert Aaron

Ben Brown was born on 1892 in London. He grew up in an upper class neighbourhood. has spent his working life in retail, starting off at John Lewis, followed by Fox’s Music as a piano specialist and demonstrator
